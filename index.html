RISOGRAPHY

The Risograph is a stencil duplicator machine that works in a similar way to screen
printing. It creates a stencil from banana paper, this is then wrapped around an ink
drum and soy ink is pushed through the voids of the stencil. As the paper glides
over the rotating drum it imprints your artwork onto its surface. The colours are
then layered up to create your masterpiece!
Riso is the only environmentally friendly form of printing and when you work with
Dopple you should know that's taken seriously. Dopple only stocks 100% recycled,
unbleached paper and compostable packaging.¬†Everything in the studio is reused
and recycled at every opportunity! Hence all the Ink canister lamps!¬†
